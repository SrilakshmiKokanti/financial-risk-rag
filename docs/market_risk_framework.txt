MARKET RISK FRAMEWORK - Version 2.1 | March 2024

1. INTRODUCTION
Market risk is the risk of losses from movements in market prices.
Covers: equity, interest rate, FX, and commodity risk.

2. VALUE AT RISK (VaR)
Methodology: Historical simulation (500-day lookback)
Confidence: 99% | Holding period: 1-day and 10-day
Limit: $50M (1-day, 99%)

3. STRESSED VaR
Based on 2008 financial crisis scenario. Limit: $120M.

4. INTEREST RATE RISK
DV01 limit: $2M per basis point.
Trading book max duration: 3 years. Banking book: 7 years.

5. FX RISK
Net open position: max 15% of Tier 1 capital.
Overnight FX positions require prior Market Risk desk approval.

6. EQUITY RISK
Single stock: max 5% of trading book NAV.
Beta-adjusted exposure limit: 1.2x benchmark.

7. STRESS TESTS (Quarterly)
- Yield curve shift: +/- 200bps
- Equity crash: -30%
- FX dislocation: +/- 20% major pairs

8. GOVERNANCE
Market Risk Committee: bi-weekly.
Breaches reported in 2h, escalated to CRO in 24h.
