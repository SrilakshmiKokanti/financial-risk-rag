CREDIT RISK POLICY - Version 3.2 | January 2024

1. OVERVIEW
Credit risk is the potential loss from a borrower failing to meet its obligations.
This policy establishes the framework for identifying, measuring, and controlling credit risk.

2. CREDIT RISK APPETITE
Target NPL ratio: no more than 2.5% of total loan portfolio.

3. CREDIT LIMITS
Single Obligor: max 10% of Tier 1 capital.
Sector Concentration: max 25% of total portfolio per industry.
Geographic: domestic exposures max 70% of total portfolio.

4. CREDIT ASSESSMENT (above $500,000)
- 3 years audited financial statements
- Credit score minimum: 650
- Collateral valuation by approved appraiser
- Senior Credit Officer approval

5. RISK MITIGATION
Accepted collateral: real estate (LTV max 75%), marketable securities (haircut 20%),
investment-grade guarantees.

6. PROVISIONING (IFRS 9 ECL)
Stage 1: 12-month ECL (performing)
Stage 2: Lifetime ECL (significant deterioration)
Stage 3: Lifetime ECL (credit impaired)
